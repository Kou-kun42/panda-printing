What is Panda Printing?

The goal of Panda Printing is to extend the tools and resources of 3D printing to those who would not have access to it otherwise. 3D printing has advanced significantly over the recent years. Users are now able to create complex shapes and stuctures that were not possible in the past with conventional techninques such as wood and metalworking, with the added benefit of being low cost. While 3D printing has become more affordable, there is still a large learning curve required to be able to use and maintain a 3D printer which can produce consistant, high quality prints. At Panda Printing, we understand that not everyone has the time or capital to invest in buying and caring for a 3D printer, and that's OK! Panda Printing is designed to quickly and efficiently put this powerful technology into the hands of the end user. Learn more about the specifics of our 3D printing solution below!

Our Printing Solution

Currently Panda Printing only has one printer, however we plan to expand that in the future. Our main printer is an Ender 3 Pro fitted with several modifications that allow for the best quality prints. The printer has a maximum print size of 220mm x 220mm x 250mm. This printer is equipped with a bowden style extruder using low tolerance Capricorn tubing that greatly reduces stringing. It also has an SKR Mini E3 control board with onboard TMC2209 stepper motor drivers that allow for higher precision and faster print speeds. Manually leveling a print bed can be inconsistent and time consuming. This is why our solution uses a BLTouch auto leveling sensor which operates before every print to create the perfect first layer, everytime. Finally, our printer has the latest Hero Me style hotend cooling system that enables far steeper supportless printing angles than the stock setup.
